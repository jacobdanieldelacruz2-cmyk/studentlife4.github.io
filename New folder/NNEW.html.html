"When the Bell Rings, Life Begins"

By Group 2

The First Bell

The school bell rang loudly, and the sound reverberated throughout the school. Today marked the start of learning, making friends, and realizing our dreams.

Initially, school was intimidating. All were seeking to see where they fit.

Lessons Beyond Books

Friendships began to emerge in group work, in snacks, and in quiet talks. The classroom became a place of instruction, as well as laughter, trust, and memory.

We gained patience during quizzes, the boldness to speak, and kindness whenever we helped each other out.
